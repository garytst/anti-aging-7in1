💰 減低香港醫療成本的方法 Ways to Reduce HK Medical Costs

香港醫療費用高昂，以下是創辦人研究出的省錢方法，讓健康保養不再是奢侈品。
Hong Kong medical costs are high. Here are practical ways to maintain health without breaking the bank.

  • 善用政府醫療券 — 每年 $2,000 醫療券可用於中醫、物理治療等預防性保健
  • Use Government Health Vouchers — Annual $2,000 HKD voucher for preventive care (TCM, physiotherapy)
  • iHerb / 海外直購 — 同款保健品比本地藥房便宜 30-60%，直送香港免運費
  • iHerb / Overseas Direct Purchase — Same supplements 30-60% cheaper than local pharmacies, free shipping to HK
  • Costco / 山姆會員店 — 大包裝保健品性價比極高，適合長期服用者
  • Costco / Sam's Club — Bulk supplements offer excellent value for long-term users
  • 善用醫管局專科門診 — 慢性病患者可以低成本獲得專科跟進
  • HA Specialist Clinics — Chronic disease patients can get specialist follow-up at minimal cost
  • 預防勝於治療 — 每日保健投資遠低於日後的醫療開支
  • Prevention Over Cure — Daily health investment is far cheaper than future medical bills
