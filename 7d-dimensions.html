🔬 Core Principle: "Natural Bypass" 核心原理「生理搭橋」

Sequential Compression 序貫加壓

During cardiac diastole, pneumatic cuffs inflate sequentially from calves → thighs → buttocks, creating powerful retrograde aortic blood flow. Coronary perfusion pressure increases by up to 93%.

心臟舒張期氣囊由小腿→大腿→臀部依次加壓，形成強大逆向主動脈血流，冠狀動脈灌注壓提升高達 93%。

Shear Stress 剪切應力

Accelerated blood flow creates powerful shear stress on vessel walls. Upper limb arteries increase by 75%, lower limb arteries by 402%, activating endothelial cells to produce beneficial substances — equivalent to "passive high-intensity exercise".

加速血流在血管壁產生強大剪切應力，上肢動脈增加 75%，下肢動脈增加 402%，激活內皮細胞，相當於「被動高強度運動」。

NO Release 一氧化氮釋放

Activates endothelial eNOS enzyme, releasing nitric oxide (NO) — the body's most powerful natural vasodilator — to dilate blood vessels system-wide, improving blood flow to every organ.

激活內皮 eNOS 酶，釋放人體最強天然血管擴張劑一氧化氮（NO），全身性擴張血管，改善所有器官血流。

Ghost Vessel Angiogenesis 幽靈血管增生

Long-term stimulation promotes VEGF release, awakening dormant "ghost vessels" and building entirely new collateral circulation — the body's own natural bypass surgery.

長期刺激促進 VEGF 釋放，喚醒休眠的「幽靈血管」，建立全新側支循環 = 身體的天然搭橋手術。

Infrared Therapy — Microcirculation Enhancement

完成「最後一公里」配送 | The Last-Mile Delivery System

🔬 Scientific Mechanism 科學機制

Photothermal Effect 光熱效應

Thermal action from infrared light dilates capillaries, improving peripheral blood perfusion. This ensures that the oxygen-rich blood from EECP and mHBOT reaches the smallest vessels.

紅外線的熱效應擴張毛細血管，改善外周血液灌注，確保EECP和mHBOT產生的富氧血液到達最小的血管。

Photobiomodulation (PBM) 光生物調節

Near-infrared light (630-1000nm) is absorbed by mitochondrial Cytochrome C Oxidase (CCO), directly boosting ATP production by 30-50%. More ATP means more cellular energy for repair.

近紅外線被線粒體細胞色素C氧化酶（CCO）吸收，直接提升ATP產量30-50%。更多ATP意味著更多細胞修復能量。

Local NO Release 局部NO釋放

Infrared light photolyzes NO bound to CCO, releasing it locally to further dilate microvessels. This creates a targeted vasodilation effect at the tissue level.

紅外線光解CCO上結合的NO，局部釋放以進一步擴張微血管。在組織層面產生靶向血管擴張效果。
